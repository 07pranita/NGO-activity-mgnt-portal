Happy to Help, Kolhapur, India.


Happy to Help is an independent charitable organisation working to better the lives of low income, informal communities in rural area of Kolhapur, India.

Happy to Help works with participants in and around Kolhapur through a range of society welfare programmes encompassing education, disaster relief, environmental and womenâ€™s empowerment.
